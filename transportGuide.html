Transport Emissions Guide

Different transport modes produce different amounts of carbon emissions per passenger kilometer. Choosing trains, buses, or bicycles significantly reduces emissions compared to cars and flights.

  • Car ≈ 120 g CO₂/km
  • Bus ≈ 70 g CO₂/km
  • Train ≈ 35 g CO₂/km
  • Plane ≈ 255 g CO₂/km
  • Bicycle ≈ near zero

Public transport can reduce travel emissions dramatically. For example, switching from car to train can cut emissions by around 80%.
